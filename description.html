BVoc in Graphics and Animation

The BVoc in Animation and Graphics is a unique program that combines the best of both worlds: animation and graphics. This course is designed to provide students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the dynamic and ever-evolving field of animation and graphics. Students will learn the fundamentals of animation, graphic design, and visual effects, as well as advanced techniques and software used in the industry. The program is designed to be hands-on and practical, with a focus on real-world applications and industry-standard tools and techniques.
